Solar Water Heater:

Solar water heating systems include storage tanks and solar collectors. There are two types of solar water heating systems: active, which have circulating pumps and controls, and passive, which don’t. The active system with “ direct circulating System” is mostly used in climates where it rarely freezes. An innovative solution aimed at ensuring a quality city with clean and jam free sewer lines. The efficient functioning of sewer systems is a major challenge faced by industries/group housing societies and cities worldwide, particularly during the rainy seasons when blockages and overflows can wreak havoc on public health, the environment, and urban infrastructure.

The sewer monitoring system utilizes a combination of advanced sensors, data analytics, and communication technologies to gather real time data from various points with in the sewer network. These sensors can measure crucial parameters like flow rates, water levels, pressure, temperature, pH levels and even detect the presence of harmful substances and pollutants. The collected data is then transmitted to a central control center or cloud-based platform for analysis and decision-making.
